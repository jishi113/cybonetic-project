About Us

Cybonetic Technologies Private Limited is a full-service IT company and digital marketing agency headquartered in Patna, Bihar. Founded in 2020, we combine deep technical expertise with data-driven marketing intelligence to deliver digital solutions that generate measurable business impact.

Our 50+ strong team of developers, designers, AI specialists and digital marketers has successfully delivered 200+ projects for clients in India, USA, UK, Canada and UAE — across 15+ industries. We are 5-star rated and ISO-compliant.

At Cybonetic, we believe in nurturing the next generation of tech talent. Our structured internship program transforms curious beginners into confident, job-ready developers through hands-on experience and real-world project delivery.

Our mission: To power the digital future by engineering digital experiences that scale.
